Biography

I am a scientific researcher specializing in botany, plant systematics, and computational evolutionary tools.

My work integrates traditional botanical taxonomy with interactive software design, aiming to make species identification accessible, dynamic, and reproducible on a global scale.

💡 Featured Tool: I design custom digital diagnostic interfaces—such as the Acalypha Species Identification System—to help experts and enthusiasts resolve plant ID bottlenecks dynamically.

Through coding and field collection, I build tools that democratize taxonomic expertise and analyze critical biogeographical patterns across complex global plant families.

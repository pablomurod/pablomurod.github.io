Biography

I am a scientific researcher specializing in botany, plant systematics, and computational evolutionary tools.

My research integrates traditional botanical taxonomy with interactive software design, making species identification accessible, dynamic, and computational.

I focus on creating interactive digital diagnostic systems—such as the Acalypha Species Identification System—to help researchers and enthusiasts identify complex plant groups dynamically.
